The GeoTag-X pilot project will teach volunteers to perform more in-depth analyses of the media (photos, video, and audio) coming out of a disaster, analyses that would usually require expert skills or experience. Anyone can become a volunteer. Download the Firefox plugin and send us relevant pictures that you find as you browse the internet, or become a part of the community by creating an account on this website.

On this website you will find applications covering a variety of different topics being deployed for different projects that UNOSAT is working on. We are still developing and testing these applications, right now they are all in demo mode, but we really need feedback from volunteers to help us improve them and make them operational.
